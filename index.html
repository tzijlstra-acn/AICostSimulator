      The full cost model — explained

      Fixed platform costs
      A one-off engineering lift to build the shared backbone: RAG pipeline, vector database, orchestration layer, and connectors to your source systems. This is amortised over the go-live timeline you set — longer runway = lower annual charge. Modelled via a Gaussian bell curve so spend peaks in the middle years of the build, not year one.
      Run costs
      Annual hosting, security, MLOps tooling, and infrastructure. Depends on your hosting choice: on-prem requires GPU cluster capex; cloud sovereign is OPEX but higher unit rate; standard cloud is cheapest. These are recurring and do not amortise.
      Licence costs
      Per-user-per-year seat charges for your AI platform layer (e.g. Copilot Studio, ServiceNow AI, or a custom licence). Scales linearly with the number of active users across all selected scenarios. Price adjusts with the annual licence growth rate you set.
      Usage costs (token spend)
      Pay-per-use inference charges: input tokens (context + retrieval) × input price, plus output tokens × output price. Driven by: requests per user per month × users × tokens per request. The token price trajectory you set (−40 % to +12 %/yr) compounds annually, making this the most uncertain line but also the one most likely to fall.
      Adoption ramp
      Users do not adopt on day one. The model applies 40 % utilisation in the go-live year, 75 % the following year, and 100 % from year 3 onward. This suppresses usage costs in early years and is why the cash-flow curve rises before it flattens.
      Division breadth share
      Fixed platform and run costs are shared across however many divisions you specify. Your slice = total ÷ number of divisions. Changing this number is the single biggest lever on your fixed-cost line.
      Scenario scaling
      Each scenario has a distinct user count and request volume. The model sums across all selected scenarios rather than applying a single percentage. Deselect low-volume scenarios to see the impact on usage costs immediately.
